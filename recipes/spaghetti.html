Spaghetti and Meatballs

"Wow, I love spaghetti." -Italians probably

Spaghetti and meatballs is an Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti, tomato sauce and meatballs. It is very delicious and today we will show you how to make your very own from scratch!

Ingredients

  • Bowl of spaghetti
  • Meatballs
  • Tomato or marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Spread tomato sauce over plate of spaghetti
  2. Put meatballs on top
  3. CONSUME
